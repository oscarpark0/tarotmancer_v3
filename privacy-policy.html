Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

Introduction

Tarotmancer ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Tarot Reading Data: When you request an AI interpretation, the cards in your spread are sent to our servers to generate your personalized reading. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored after your reading is complete.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We store minimal data locally on your device to manage app functionality, such as timestamps for rate limiting features. This data never leaves your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media from your device
  • Browsing history
  • Financial information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Generate personalized tarot interpretations based on your card spreads
  • Manage the 24-hour interpretation limit feature
  • Improve app performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Rate limiting data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms.
  • Server Processing: Card spread data sent for AI interpretation is processed in real-time and is not retained on our servers after the interpretation is delivered.
  • No Account Required: Tarotmancer does not require user accounts, and we do not store any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Tarotmancer uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic API: To generate AI-powered tarot interpretations. Card spread data is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

Tarotmancer is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. Local app data can be cleared by uninstalling the app from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Consent
